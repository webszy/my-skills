## Acquisition

Summarize source, campaign, country, store conversion, cost, and install/download quality.

## Activation

Summarize launch, onboarding, permission, core feature, result, completion, paywall, and ad interruption paths.

## Retention

Summarize cohort retention, return usage, and whether the core feature is repeated.

## Ad Monetization

Summarize ad revenue by ad unit, format, country, network, eCPM, CTR, fill/show rate, and code placement.

## Subscription

Summarize paywall views, trials, purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, package performance, and entitlement logic.

## Country / Channel Quality

Compare acquisition quality, activation, revenue, retention, stability, and ROAS by country or channel.

## Stability

Summarize crash-free users/sessions, fatal/non-fatal issues, affected users, versions, devices, and core-flow impact.
